## What Makes A Marketing Image Actually Useful

A marketing image should not just be visually interesting. It should support a business goal.

That usually means:

- clear focal point
- easy brand alignment
- usable negative space for copy
- clean composition
- enough realism or polish for the channel

GPT Image 2 workflows are more valuable when they help create visuals with those traits, not just artistic variety.

## A Practical Workflow

Marketing teams usually get better results with this sequence:

1. define the campaign message
2. write one precise visual brief
3. generate several base concepts
4. select one strong direction
5. refine it through image editing
6. export channel-ready variants

This is more effective than repeatedly generating unrelated prompts.
